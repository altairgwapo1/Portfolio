Journal

UP in IT Cebu Information Technoloy Center

UP in IT Cebu refers to the strong Information Technology and Computer Science programs at the University of the Philippines (UP) Cebu, located in Lahug, Cebu City, known for its excellent Computer Science department recognized as a National Center of Excellence, offering research-focused MSCS and undergraduate programs.

Dynata Philippines Inc

Dynata Cebu is the local office of Dynata, a global leader in first-party data for market research, operating from Lapu-Lapu City, Cebu, Philippines, providing data insights and powering research for brands, agencies, and researchers worldwide.

Rivan IT Cebu

Rivan IT Cebu is a Philippine-based IT training center offering hands-on bootcamps in in-demand technologies like networking, VoIP, fiber optics, and Linux, using real equipment. It provides beginner-to-advanced training with experienced instructors, high certification passing rates, and career-focused programs for local and overseas IT jobs.

MATA technologies

Mata Technologies Inc., a Filipino tech company that creates immersive virtual reality (VR) experiences, specializing in virtual maps and 360° tours for Philippine tourism and real estate. They use technology to bring locations like tourist spots, heritage sites, and properties to life online, making them accessible through interactive, VR-powered exploration, with goals to boost tourism and business.

T.A.R.S.I.E.R. 117

TaRSIER 117 (Telephone and Radio System Integrated Emergency Response) is Bohol Province's official emergency hotline and disaster management system, similar to 911, providing 24/7 coordination for police, medical, and fire emergencies, disaster preparedness, and public advisories using radio and phone networks for swift, life-saving responses in Bohol, Philippines.
